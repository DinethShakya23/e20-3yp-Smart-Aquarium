Smart Aquarium Tank 🚰🐟

This project focuses on developing an intelligent aquarium system with various automated and monitoring functionalities to ensure a healthy and convenient environment for aquatic life.

Key Features 🌟

  • 🧪 Measure pH Value
    Continuously monitor the pH level of the water to ensure optimal conditions for fish and aquatic plants.
  • 🌡️ Temperature Monitoring & Control
    Keep track of water temperature and optionally manage heating or cooling systems for precise thermal regulation.
  • 🍴 Automatic Feeding System
    Feed the fish at scheduled intervals or based on manual triggers, reducing the hassle of daily feeding.
  • 🐠🔍 Fish Activity Tracking
    Utilize OpenCV to analyze fish activity, detect unusual behavior, and provide insights into their health and well-being.

Tech Stack 💻

This project integrates IoT sensors, automation modules, and computer vision techniques to provide real-time monitoring and control through a connected system.

Developed with ❤️ for a smarter aquatic experience.
